What is Cops and Robbers (CNR)?

CNR is a gamemode that turns the childhood game we all love into a virtual depiction where you flee from the cops! There's no requirement to be either - You can just join the game, play in our events, drive vehicles, or complete missions and avoid the cops altogether if you wish.

So this is a Deathmatch Server?

No. It is not allowed for anyone to just randomly kill another player. It's encouraged to try to escape police without resorting to murder, however, deathmatch between a Cop and a Wanted is generally tolerated.

I'm a first time player, how do I start?

Simply click 'PLAY', and you'll be taken to the one-time character designer. From then on, you'll simply reconnect where ever you last logged off the next time you click 'PLAY'. Your info is saved based on your Steam ID, so you can play from anywhere you can access your Steam Account.

How do I become a Cop?

Simply visit any police department in the active area of the map to go on duty at any time. More duty privileges will come as you gain levels in Law Enforcement.

How do I lose my wanted level?

Short of giving yourself up to police, you lose wanted points every minute that you're not actively being chased by police. Once your Wanted Points are 0, you are Unwanted. If you're actively being chased, you will not lose Wanted Points. Lose the cops to start losing Wanted Points.
