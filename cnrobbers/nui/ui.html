What is Cops and Robbers (CNR)?

CNR is a gamemode that turns the childhood game we all love into a virtual depiction where you flee from the cops! There's no requirement to be either - You can just join the game, play in our events, drive vehicles, or complete missions and avoid the cops altogether if you wish.

So this is a Deathmatch Server?

No. It is not allowed for anyone to just randomly kill another player. It's encouraged to try to escape police without resorting to murder, however, deathmatch between a Cop and a Wanted is generally tolerated.

I'm a first time player, how do I start?

Simply click 'PLAY', and you'll be taken to the one-time character designer. From then on, you'll simply reconnect where ever you last logged off the next time you click 'PLAY'. Your info is saved based on your Steam ID, so you can play from anywhere you can access your Steam Account.

How do I become a Cop?

Simply visit any police department in the active area of the map to go on duty at any time. More duty privileges will come as you gain levels in Law Enforcement.

How do I lose my wanted level?

Short of giving yourself up to police, you lose wanted points every minute that you're not actively being chased by police. Once your Wanted Points are 0, you are Unwanted. If you're actively being chased, you will not lose Wanted Points. Lose the cops to start losing Wanted Points.

Gamemode Information

5MCNR is a game of, well, cops and robbers. The whole point is to chase/become wanted, or stay innocent, and earn as much money as possible to fund your addictions. Buy cars, a house, an apartment, a business, weapon upgrades, vehicle upgrades, and other things like drugs and women (or men?). Your criminal history doesn't follow you, so do whatever you want. Just don't die.

Oh, also, try not to die without health insurance.. You'll lose all your money, weapons, owned vehicles, and everything. Yes, you'll lose your house and everything that was in it, too. So don't die without health insurance. Ahhh... America.

Legal Jobs / Activites

Around the map you'll find various jobs you can do, side jobs, and activities. This secton outlines the legal side of this.

Legal Employment

Visit any location on the map with a briefcase to get a job. The map blip will tell you what the job is when you hover it on the map. All you need to know to start the job, is to walk into the golden circle and press interacton (E by default). The job will literally tell you what to do next.

Fishing

Fishing is boring but it's an easy way to make money in between jobs, or to break up the monotony of running delivery jobs. Follow this extremely detailed guide on how to get started fishing.

  • Buy a Fishing Rod & Bait from 24/7
  • Find Water
  • Stick Feet in Water or get on a boat
  • Open Inventory (F1)
  • Select Fishing Rod & click "Use"
  • Play the minigame until you're done
  • Take your fish to the Fish Market for cash
Hunting

Hunting is a great way to slaughter animals for profit. Simply find anything with four legs that you think PETA would get pissed off about, and kill it. Once it's dead, take out a knife, and skin it. PETA might shut our server down though if you try skinning it before you kill it, so kill it first. Once you stop failing and actually skin something successfully, you can sell the hides at the Market (pink cash sign).

It goes without saying that hunting domestic dogs, endangered animals or strippers will prooooobably net you a wanted level..

Drug/Weapons Trafficking

Crates spawn randomly all around the map. These crates contain things like drug parephenalia, gun parts, illegal vehicle mods, dirty cash, and much more. When one becomes available, an anonymous text message will alert all players about it, and a red circle will appear on the map. This circle is different for everyone, so if you can put aside your greediness, working with other people can help you find it easier. Look for a crate that's out of place. When you press interact (E by default) on the crate, it will disappear and the items will be placed into your inventory.

Information on being a Cop

Arresting Someone

Tase them. Seriously. That's it. Run up to them and make them twitch. While they're spazing out, they'll be sent to jail. If they're not sent to jail, congratulations, you just committed police brutality!

Issuing a Ticket

If a player's name is light-yellow, in other words, their wanted level is lower than 4, you'll have to issue them a ticket. Instead of getting Internal Affairs excited sexually and tasing them, press F2 (default) to issue them a ticket. Wait to see if they pay it. If you issue a ticket and drive off so they become wanted, you're an asshole and we're going to kick you off the server for it. Be a good sport, not an asshole.

Getting a Vehicle

Cops can only drive cop cars (What!). Head to the garage icon at any active police station to pick a vehicle. Anytime you're outside, you can also use /copcar to spawn a car incase you're stranded. However, this command can only be used once every 5 minutes, notifies the admins that you used it, and only spawns a basic Crown Victoria.

Housing / Apartments

Not available yet. Enjoy being homeless.

Death is Inevitable

Everyone's going to die one day. Well, on 5MCNR, you'll probably die multiple times a day. So get health insurance. Just like real life, our game follows capitalist healthcare. If you don't have the money, you're going to die and lose everything. Buy health insurance at any hospital by pressing interact (E default) in the white/pink circle.

Passive Mode

Passive Mode is enabled when you die, for 5 minutes. If you take hostile action like equipping a gun, committing a crime, or going on police duty, passive mode will be removed. The entire point of passive mode is to avoid spawn campers from griefing your insurance premiums. It's not so you can walk around peacefully. It's not so you can roleplay with your buddy. It's so you don't get camped at the emergency room. That's it.

Dying Without Insurance

If you die without insurance you lose everything except the points and ranks you built up in game. You'll retain the ranks needed to checkout high speed police cars or buy powerful weapons, but your entire belongings and assets are lost.

Lottery System

We have a lottery system! Queue gambling addiction. There are two types of lottery systems in place currently.

Scratchers

You can play scratchers at anytime. Visit 24/7, buy as many scratchers as you want, and then select them in your inventory and click "USE". Once scratched, if it wins, you'll be given a "Winning Scratcher" item, where the quantity you have is the value of the win. Simply visit any 24/7 and when you open the store menu, it'll cash in any winning cards.

Hourly Drawing

Buy a lotto ticket at any 24/7, and then "use" it from your Inventory. Once used, you'll be prompted to choose a number. Pick the number you want to play, and if the number's available, you'll be in for the draw. You do not have to be connected to win. If you win offline, the money will be placed into your account. If the number doesn't work or has already been chosen, you'll be refunded the ticket and be able to choose again.

If you die, you guessed it. Your lotto number is forfeited.

Getting Caught

TICKETS: If you're at Wanted Levels 1-3, then an officer can only issue you a ticket. If you fail to pay this ticket, you'll be bumped up to Wanted Level 4, and then you can be sent to jail. A cop who kills you at this wanted level will be punished, unless you have a weapon in your hand.

JAIL: If tased, you'll be sent to the Mission Row PD Jail. Once your time is up you'll be moved out automatically. You can also try to /bribe a nearby cop, but beware that refused bribes will result in a wanted level. You can also /lockpick the door, if you bought lockpicks at 24/7. Just be aware if you break someone out of jail, or you break out of jail yourself, you'll be charged automatically after 1 minute with jailbreak, making you a felon.

PRISON: If tased, you'll be sent to the Mission Row PD Jail. Once your time is up you'll be moved out automatically. You can also try to /bribe a nearby cop, but beware that refused bribes will result in a wanted level. You can also /lockpick the door, if you bought lockpicks at 24/7.

The "guards" check the jail/prison every 2 minutes - If you're not there when they do their checks, you'll be charged with jailbreak.

Losing Wanted Levels

Your wanted level goes down naturally if there are no cops nearby. So stay away from the fuzz and you can actually lose your wanted level.
